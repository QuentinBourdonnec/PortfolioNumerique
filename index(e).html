Robot Firefighter

Réalisation d'un mini programme Robot Pompier en Langage C.

Project of 2 weeks concerning the realization of a program in c to play on the terminal, without vertitables tracks with the realization. Only a notebook Loads was given as well as several game cards with different difficulty levels. The aim was to create a robot that Go to extinguish a fire by retrieving a fire extinguisher. But the robot knew neither the position of the fire nor the fire extinguisher. It was therefore necessary to make sure that it determines the shortest way and to retain the position of the fire if it found no position of the front light That of the extinguisher. Added many other options to increase the difficulty and customization of the game by adding life bar, Of color ..

Overall image of the game, the game undergoes an update every half second to have a motion effect

10 color life gauge depending on number of remaining life

Game in terminal mode, the start is marked by a D (green), the robot by an R (blue), the multicolor fire (white) and the fire extinguisher represented by an E (red)
